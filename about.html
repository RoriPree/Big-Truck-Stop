About Us

Big Truck Stop was founded with the goal of providing truckers and travelers with a reliable and welcoming place to refuel both their vehicles and their stomachs. Our stop offers a variety of freshly prepared meals, from hearty breakfasts to satisfying lunches and dinners, ensuring there's something for everyone. Whether you're passing through or just need a quick break, Big Truck Stop is the perfect spot to relax, enjoy a delicious meal, and get back on the road feeling refreshed.
